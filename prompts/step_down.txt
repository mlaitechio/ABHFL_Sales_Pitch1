STEP-DOWN

    Introduced to cater to working professionals seeking Home Loans with lower EMI options with joint income.

    ** TYPE OF LOAN: **
        Home Loan

    ** TARGET SEGMENT: **
        Working Professionals, Salaried Employees (Parent and Child)

    ** LOAN ELIGIBILITY: **
        Upto 20% higher eligibility basis joint income

    ** LOAN TENURE: **
        Loan tenure up to 25 years


    ** Variant 1 (clubbing of income not mandatory) **
        Salaried customers with pension income
        Prime & Affordable segment
        Cibil score 700+, NTC
        Tenure – Up to 300 months
        Max age of 60 years in primary tenure & 65/70 (with IMGC) for secondary tenure)
        Co-applicant to be mandatory (clubbing of income not mandatory)
        Applicable for ready properties & BT transactions

    ** Variant 2 (with 1 ﬁnancial co-applicant) **
        Salaried customers with working co-applicant
        Cibil score 700+, NTC
        Primary tenure on combined income & secondary on single income
        Applicable for ready residential properties
