[Insurance]{.underline}


> *Property Insurance*
>
> Borrower has to get all the properties offered as collateral insured.
> The insurer is expected to cover the following risk:-

-   Risk from Fire, Earthquake, flood or other nature calamity

-   The insurance policy so taken should be endorsed in favour of ABHFL.

-   Insurance should cover the reconstruction cost as mentioned in
> valuation report.

> *Life /Health /General Insurance*
>
> It is preferable that all promoter individual involved in managing
> affairs of business and key figure therein avails term cover linked to
> the loan covering life / health and other risk to the extent of
> amortized outstanding loan through the tenor of loan proposed.
