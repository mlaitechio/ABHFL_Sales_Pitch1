> *[For Calculation Disbursal Amount based on % Disbursement
> Recommended]{.underline}*
>
> Below mentioned calculations are to be followed for arriving at the
> Disbursal Amount. In case, amount requested is greater than calculated
> amount deviation needs to be marked in the appraisal.

-   Disbursal Amount = (Cost of Property (COP) x % Recommended by the
> Technical Agency) -- OCR

-   OCR (Own Contribution Receipt) = COP -- Loan Amount
