LEASE RENTAL DISCOUNTING (LRD)

**Loan Amount:**
- Minimum â†’ 50 lacs - CAT A/B/C
- Maximum:
  - CAT A/B (Commercial/Industrial Property) - No Limit
  - CAT C (Only Commercial Property) - 5 Cr
  - CAT D (Only Commercial Property) - 3 Cr

### Loan Tenure:

- CAT A & B - Upto 15 years or unexpired lease /LOI period whichever is earlier
- CAT C/D - Upto 10 years or unexpired lease /LOI period whichever is earlier

### NPV%:

- 90% of Net rent (Post TDS & Taxes effect) - CAT A/B/C
- 80% of Net rent (Post TDS & Taxes effect) - CAT D

### LTV:


| Property Type  | CAT-A                                        | CAT-B                                        | CAT-C               | CAT-D                   |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------- | ----------------------- |
| **Commercial** | 75% for up to 10 Cr.<br>70% for above 10 Cr. | 70% for up to 10 Cr.<br>65% for above 10 Cr. | 65% for loan amount | 65% for all loan amount |
| **Industrial** | 55%                                          | 55%                                          | NA                  | NA                      |
| **Warehouse**  | 65%                                          | 65%                                          | NA                  | NA                      |


CIBIL Score:

CIBIL Score of NTC/730 +

### Lessees Acceptance Criteria:

Companies/Partnership/LLP/Pvt. Firm meeting below criteria:

| CAT Type | Turnover | PAT | Net Worth | Vintage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| CAT A Indian | >=500 Cr | +ve | +ve | - |
| CAT A MNC* | >= 5 billion USD | >=200 million in India | - | 24 months |
| CAT B Indian | <500 - >=250 Crs | +ve | +ve | - |
| CAT B MNC* | >= 5 billion USD | >=200 million in India | - | 12 months |
| CAT C Indian | <250 - >=100 Crs | +ve | +ve | - |
| CAT D Indian | <100 - >=50 Crs | +ve | +ve | 10 years |

*Indian subsidiary/franchisee (operating for more than 2 years) of an MNC with Equity in subsidiary >= 50% (this could be less than 50% if specific sector FDI limits are regulated/restricted due to government policy e.g. Insurance sector)
