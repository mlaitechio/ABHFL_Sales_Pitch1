# **Semi-Fixed Rate of Interest Offering**

This product is designed for customers who want stability **and** flexibility. It is suitable for **salaried individuals** and **small business owners** (e.g., food stalls, tea shops, vegetable/fruit vendors, and similar setups).

### **Core Features**

| Feature              | Details                                                                 |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
