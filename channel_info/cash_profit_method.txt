 CASH PROFIT METHOD (CPM)

**Loan Amount:**
- Minimum → 10L
- Maximum → 15 CR

**FOIR:** Max 80%

**LTV:** As Per Product

**Applicable for SENP Customers**

### Eligibility Computation:

**Eligible Income =** Core Income + EBITDA + Income from House Property/Rental Income + Other Income

### Income Consideration Norms:

**Salary Income:**
- Net salary as per ITR → 100%

**EBITDA:**
- PBT + Depreciation + Salary or Interest paid to Directors or Partners → 100%

**Interest Paid:**
- 100%

**Income from House Property/Rentals:**
- 100%

### EBITDA Norms:

- EBITDA data is to be taken from latest financials as filed with IT Body / Audit Reports

**Growth / Decline in EBITDA in current year vis-à-vis previous year:**

- Where Current Year EBITDA % growth is up to 50% of the previous year EBITDA, Current Year EBITDA is to be considered
- Where Current Years EBITDA % growth is >50% of the last year EBITDA, average of Last 2 year's or 50% of growth over previous year EBITDA whichever is higher
- Approval is required for >20% decline in EBITDA

### Other Income Norms:

- 100% Avg Agricultural Income of last 2 years, as per ITR
- 100% Avg income of the last 2 years from Other Sources as per ITR
- Other Income capped at 100% of (Salary Income + EBITDA)

### Interest Norms:

- Interest paid to close family/relatives can be considered, as reflecting in 40 A (2) (b) as per tax audit report
- Interest on CC/OD is neither to be added back nor it is to be considered as obligation
