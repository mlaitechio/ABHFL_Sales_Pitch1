HL PLUS PRAGATI PLUS

### What is it all about?

Introduced to cater to cash rich self employed customers & Prime salaried customers looking to purchase property with minor deviations.

### Product Details:

**Type of Loan:** HL Builder Purchase / Resale, Plot Purchase & Construction, Home construction loan, Home Extension Loan, Home Improvement Loan

**Target Segment:**
- Segment 1: SENP with assessed income purchasing standard property (Type I)
- Segment 2: Bank Salaried customers purchasing Type II property

**Max Loan Amount:** Segment 1: Cat A+/A locations – 1 Cr
Segment 2: Cat A+/A locations – 1.5 Cr


**Loan Tenure:**
- Minimum: 12 months
- Maximum: 240 months for Self Employed & 360 months for Salaried

**Eligible Products:** HL, P+C, HCL, HEL, HIL

**Targeted Yield:** 10% - 11.5%
