MICRO CF (CONSTRUCTION FINANCE)

### What is it all about?

Introduced to cater to affordable developers looking to develop small size projects and looking for financial support.

### Product Details:

**Type of Loan:** Term Loan provided for Construction

**Target Segment:** Affordable Developers, Small Scale Builders

**Ticket Size:** Loan amount up to 5 Crores

**Loan Tenure:** Option 1: up to 5 years (including 1 year of principal moratorium)

Option 2: Up to 7 years (84 months) with EMI


**Eligible Products:** LAP – Self Occupied Residential Property & LAP – Self Occupied Commercial Property

**Targeted Yield:** 13% - 16%

Repayment mode & Moratorium period :

Option 1:
1 year principal moratorium & balance 4 years repayment tenure with equal monthly principal instalments + interest payment.

Option 2:
EMI to start from date of 1st disbursement for tenure of 7 years.

Micro CF – Detailed Norms 
Project Selection
Projects must be clear, legal, and technical.

The property must have a clear & marketable title.

Projects with maximum 10 units are permissible under this product.

The project should not be stalled for more than 3 months.

Regularized colonies and projects with road widening impact are not to be considered under this program.

Collateral Type
The collateral can be Residential or Mixed Usage.

In case of Mixed Usage, the Commercial component cannot be more than 30% of the total saleable area.

LTV Norms (Specific Calculation Rules)
The loan is to be booked under Non-Home Loan as per LTV norms.

Plot LTV is 50% on Market value or 75% of Documented value, whichever is lower.

Resale plots can be funded only when part of plotted development (with any deviation requiring approval at the NCC level).

Construction LTV is 70% of the construction cost (this cost must be prepared by a Chartered Engineer/Architect/Civil Contractor and verified by the panel valuer).

The LTV on the total property (Plot + Construction) is not to exceed 65%.

Repayment
EMI starts from the date of first disbursement.

No Pre-EMI is allowed.

The builder must give an undertaking for proportionate prepayment on the sale of every floor/unit/NOC issuance.

SI% - 75% of the unit value is to be collected at the time of NOC issuance.

Retail Conversion
ABHFL gets the first right of refusal for retail funding in the borrower project.

The builder must take NOC from ABHFL before the sale of any unit.

The target for retail HL conversion is a minimum of 50%, capped at a maximum of 80% of units.
