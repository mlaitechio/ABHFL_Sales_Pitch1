HL BOOSTER GENERAL PURPOSE LOAN

### What is it all about?

Introduced to support eligible select customers in their home buying journey with General purpose loan (For Home Improvement, Interiors) up to 15% additional loan amount.

### Product Details:

**Type of Loan:** Bundled as an offering with Home loan purchase

**Target Segment:**
- Salaried customers – ABG/CAT A/CAT B
- SEP/SENP with CIBIL score of 730+

**Ticket Size:** Loan amount up to 50 lakh (Upto 15% additional loan amount)
Max Loan Amount:
Cat A+/A: 50 lakhs
Other locations: 30 lakhs

Loan Tenure: 
Same as Home Loan tenure offered
