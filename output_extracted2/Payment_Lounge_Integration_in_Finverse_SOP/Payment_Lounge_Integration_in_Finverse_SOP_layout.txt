Payment Lounge Integration in Finverse - SOP
1. We have recently integrated Payment Lounge with Finverse, where customer payments
towards Processing fees will be routed through Payment lounge.
2. At Sourcing stage, Sales team to continue existing process, of triggering payment link to the
customer. Sourcing

Review Eligibility
→
Trigger link
